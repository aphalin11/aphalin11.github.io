My Goals

My goals are to work in a cozy place filled with nerds. I want to drink coffee on the regular and write nice code, and collaborate in team settings. I want the opportunity to explore the world of information and learn something new every day, whether it be a skill or a insight about some field in finance, technology, physics, or biology. Back at home, outside of my career I want to own a yard in which I can routinely manage and grow my own prairie, and to have some money to spare to take camping vacations and attend concerts and try lots of different craft beers.
